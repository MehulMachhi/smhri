* Food sources for Vitamin B12 (Cobalamine) are Meat, fish, poultry, eggs and dairy products (milk, cheeses and yogurt).

* Vegans (vegetarian people) are at risk of a Vitamin B12 (Cobalamine) deficiency.

* Vitamin B12 (Cobalamine) level less than 211 pg/ml, Elevated serum or urinary methylmalonate and raised plasma homocystein indicate that the    Vitamin B12 (Cobalamine) is low.

* Vitamin B12 (Cobalamine) deficiency causes megaloblastic anemia in which the erythrocytes fail to mature properly.

* Vitamin B12 (Cobalamine) deficiency also causes demyelination of the large nerve fibres of the spinal cord, especially the posterior and lateral columns    which causes loss of sansation and motor power in the lower limbs.
